Here's a comprehensive breakdown of cards that synergize with **[Isshin, Two Heavens as One](https://scryfall.com/card/aneo/14/isshin-two-heavens-as-one-isshin-two-heavens-as-one?utm_source=api)**! Isshin doubles any triggered ability that triggers when a creature attacks. Here are some great options:

## Top Synergy Cards for Isshin

### 🃏 Attack Trigger Creatures (Mardu colors)
- **[Kaalia of the Vast](https://scryfall.com/card/amh3/22/kaalia-of-the-vast-kaalia-of-the-vast?utm_source=api)** - With Isshin, you get to put TWO Angels/Demons/Dragons into play attacking per swing!
- **[Caesar, Legion's Emperor](https://scryfall.com/card/pip/1/caesar-legions-emperor?utm_source=api)** - Doubles his attack trigger, letting you choose 2+ options from his modal ability
- **[Zurgo Stormrender](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/10/zurgo-stormrender?utm_source=api)** - Creates two Warrior tokens instead of one, drawing cards when they die
- **[Bone-Cairn Butcher](https://scryfall.com/card/tdm/173/bone-cairn-butcher?utm_source=api)** - Mobilize 2 becomes Mobilize 4 (four Warrior tokens per attack), and they have deathtouch
- **[Edgar Markov](https://scryfall.com/card/ainr/24/edgar-markov-edgar-markov?utm_source=api)** - Puts two +1/+1 counters on each Vampire instead of one

### 🛠️ Equipment
- **[Sword of the Animist](https://scryfall.com/card/cmm/413/sword-of-the-animist?utm_source=api)** - Search for TWO basic lands every attack instead of one (huge ramp!)

### 🌟 Enchantments
- **[Beastmaster Ascension](https://scryfall.com/card/ncc/283/beastmaster-ascension?utm_source=api)** - Put TWO quest counters each time a creature attacks (gets to 7 counters twice as fast!)
- **[All-Out Assault](https://scryfall.com/card/atdm/43/all-out-assault-all-out-assault?utm_source=api)** - Gives +1/+1 and deathtouch, with extra combat synergy
- **[Kindred Discovery](https://scryfall.com/card/awoe/67/kindred-discovery-kindred-discovery?utm_source=api)** - Draw TWO cards per attacking creature of the chosen type instead of one

### 🏞️ Lands
- **[Minas Tirith](https://scryfall.com/card/altr/55/minas-tirith-minas-tirith?utm_source=api)** - Draws a card every attack with 2+ creatures (synergizes with Isshin if multiple creatures attacking matters)
- **[Windbrisk Heights](https://scryfall.com/card/tdc/411/windbrisk-heights?utm_source=api)** - If you attack with 3+ creatures you can play the exiled card... and with Isshin helping you make more attackers, this is easier to enable
