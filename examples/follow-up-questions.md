## 🐉 Niv-Mizzet, Visionary — Synergy Cards

**[Niv-Mizzet, Visionary](https://scryfall.com/card/fdn/123/niv-mizzet-visionary?utm_source=api)** says: *"Whenever a source you control deals noncombat damage to an opponent, you draw that many cards."* Plus *"You have no maximum hand size."*

So the name of the game is **dealing noncombat damage** to opponents (spells, abilities, trigger damage, etc.) to draw tons of cards!
